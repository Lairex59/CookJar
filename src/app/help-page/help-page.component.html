Description:

Introducing our cooking app, the ultimate solution for all your culinary needs! With our app, you can explore a vast collection of recipes sourced from the OpendataHub API. Not only that, you can also add your own favorite recipes to the app and save them locally for quick access.

But that's not all! Our app also features an innovative AI technology that lets you generate images for your recipes, making your culinary creations even more visually appealing. Plus, you can even let the AI generate entire recipes for you to try out and expand your culinary repertoire.

Whether you're a novice cook or a seasoned chef, our app has something for everyone.
